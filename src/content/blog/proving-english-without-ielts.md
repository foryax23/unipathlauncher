IELTS is still the default, but most UK universities now accept a range of alternatives. Plan early — test slots fill fast in peak season.

## Common accepted alternatives

- **TOEFL iBT** — widely accepted; check minimum sub-scores.
- **Pearson PTE Academic** — fast results, popular in Asia.
- **Duolingo English Test** — accepted by many universities, denied by some Russell Group programmes.
- **Cambridge C1 Advanced / C2 Proficiency** — lifetime validity, well respected.

## When you may not need any test

- Your medium of instruction was English for the last 2+ years (request a letter from your school).
- You hold a degree taught in English from a recognised institution.
- You're from a "majority English-speaking country" on the UKVI list.

## A simple action plan

1. Make a shortlist of five courses.
2. Read each course's English requirements page — note acceptable tests and minimum scores.
3. Book the cheapest accepted test that meets every requirement.
4. Sit a mock under timed conditions before booking the real thing.
